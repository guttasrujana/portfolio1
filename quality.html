QUALITY

To fulfill the requirement of the quality policy, quality manual is the guidance document to achieve the quality objectives.

Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) are prepared, followed and updated as per the current good manufacturing guidelines to fulfill the requirement of quality manual.

Joshika Pharma follows GMP, GLP recommendations and fulfills the standards of the Regulatory authorities. The applications of the cGMP and ISO standards, as well as the issuing and updating of all the technical documentation are coordinated by Quality Operation (Quality Unit) with the frequent internal audits.

Our quality policy covers:

  • Qualified vendors
  • Qualified equipments
  • Qualified personnel
  • Training
  • Validations
  • Qualified sampling and testing procedures
  • Equipment calibrations
  • Change control and documentation
  • ISO Approvals
